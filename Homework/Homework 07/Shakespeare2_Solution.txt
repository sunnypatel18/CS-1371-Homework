Puppet? Why so? So, that way goes the game.
Now I see that she has made a comparison
Between our heights; she has urged her height,
And with her personage, her tall personage,
Her height, indeed, she has prevailed with him.
And are you grown so high in his esteem,
Because I am so dwarfish and so short?
How short am I, you painted maypole? Speak!
How short am I? I am not yet so short
But that my nails can reach into your eyes.
